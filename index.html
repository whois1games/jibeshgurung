About Jibesh Singh Gurung

Jibesh Singh Gurung is Singer, Actor, YouTuber, script writer, and director from Nepal. He started his career as a theater actor and has since appeared in numerous Music Videos, Ads and television shows. He has also written, directed and Edited several short films. In addition to his work in the entertainment industry, Gurung is also an active philanthropist, working to raise awareness and funds for various charities.
